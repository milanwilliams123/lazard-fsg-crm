How to navigate

Eight tabs,
one connected system

Each tab in the nav covers a distinct part of the coverage workflow. Everything links — click a firm to see its contacts, click a contact to see their activity, click a deal to see the full mandate history.

◈
Dashboard
The starting point. Five stat tiles at the top are all clickable — each one expands an inline panel with the underlying records. The "Tier 1 Gone Cold" tile shows any Tier 1 firm with no touchpoint in 60+ days. "Portcos Exploring Exit" surfaces active deal opportunities. Click any row to open the full detail.
⬡
Firms
The full sponsor universe, filterable by tier and sector. Click any row to open the firm page — you'll see the firm's contacts with their last five interactions listed inline, the portco grid with exit status, and any linked deals. A yellow warning banner appears if coverage has gone stale.
◎
Contacts
All deal team contacts across every covered firm. Click a contact to see their full profile — email, phone, recent deals, and which Lazard bankers are mapped to them. From a contact page you can log a new activity directly or navigate to their firm.
⊗
Coverage Map
A visual index of the firm → contact → Lazard banker relationship. Useful for a quick audit of who owns what — scan down to find any contact without a Lazard banker mapped, or to see how coverage is distributed across the sponsor coverage team.
⊕
Activity Log
Every logged touchpoint in reverse chronological order. Filter by firm or by type (Meeting, Pitch, Call, Conference). Click any row to see the full notes, outcome, and next action. Use the "+ Log Activity" button to record a new touchpoint from scratch.
⟁
Deal Tracker
All buy-side and sell-side mandates in one place — pitches, live mandates, and closed deals. Filter by status using the buttons at the top: Pitching, Mandated, Closed Won, Lost. Click a deal card to see the full description, linked portco, buy-side firms pitched, and all associated activity.
⬕
LZ Team
The internal Lazard banker directory. Sponsor coverage MDs are listed alongside sector bankers. Click any card to open a banker's profile — their full coverage universe, recent activity history, pitch record, and which sponsor contacts are mapped to them.
⊞
Export
Select which datasets to include using the checkboxes, then click Download. The output is a formatted .xlsx workbook — Dashboard tab with live COUNTIF formulas, plus separate tabs for Activity Log, Deals, Portfolio Companies, and Lazard Team. Formatted in Lazard navy and gold.

Start here — Dashboard
Check what needs attention today
Open the CRM and the Dashboard gives you an immediate read on the state of coverage. The five tiles at the top are your daily priorities — click any one to expand the detail panel below.
  • Tier 1 Gone Cold — click to see which Tier 1 firms are overdue for a touchpoint and jump straight to their firm page
  • Pitches in Flight — click to see all active pitches with next actions listed
  • Overdue Follow-ups — any logged activity where the next action date has passed
  • Portcos Exploring Exit — click through to the portco and its linked deal
Firms → Blackstone
Firms tab → firm page
Everything about a sponsor in one view
Click any firm in the Firms tab to open its dedicated page. From here you can see the full picture of your relationship with that sponsor without navigating away.
  • Contacts are listed with their last five interactions expanded inline — no need to click through to each contact
  • The portco grid shows each portfolio company with exit status — click a portco to see its linked deal
  • A yellow stale coverage banner appears if the firm hasn't been touched in 45+ days
  • Use "+ Log Activity" at the top right to record a new touchpoint directly against this firm
Deals · Pitching / Mandated / Closed
Deals tab
Track pitches and mandates together
The Deals tab covers everything from first pitch to close in a single pipeline view. Use the status filter buttons at the top to cut the view — click "Pitching" to see only active pitches, "Mandated" for live mandates.
  • Each deal card shows the sponsor firm, linked portco, Lazard role (buy-side or sell-side), and current status
  • Click a card to open the full deal modal — description, notes, buy-side firms pitched, and all logged activity against that deal
  • The activity count on each card tells you how much relationship work has gone into it
  • Add a new deal with the "+ Add Deal" button — it will immediately appear in the pipeline and can be linked to portcos and activities
